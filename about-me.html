Nathan

My family first moved to downtown Atlanta, but we soon moved to the suburbs of Marietta to live next to my grandparents. After this moment, my family settled down in Marietta.

I was raised in Marietta with my mom, dad, and sister. I attended Sawyer Road Elementary, Marietta Center for Advanced Academics (MCAA), Marietta Sixth Grade Academy, and Marietta Middle School. I was lucky enough to make life-long friends that would be with me since elementary school. In August 2018, I became a freshman at Marietta High School, and I enjoyed my first year of high school. Jumping to the present, I am currently enrolled in the SMASH academy at Morehouse College learning about STEM. This program takes place during the summers after my 9th, 10th, and 11th grade years.
